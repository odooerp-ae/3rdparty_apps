Import Accounts from Salesforce to Odoo

  1. First, open your Salesforce account.
  2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner.
  3. Select the Compact view from the “Comfy” or “Compact” options.
  1. On the top-left corner, click the 3×3 grid (App Launcher) button.
  2. Then click View All.
  3. After that, click on Sales.
  4. At the top, you’ll see options like Home, Opportunities, Leads, Tasks, etc.
  5. Click on Accounts.
  1. Provide the necessary information.
  2. Click on Save.
  1. Now, open your Odoo instance.
  2. Navigate to the Salesforce Connector menu.
  3. Click the Import Accounts button.
  4. You will see a confirmation message with Accounts, Time, and Success status as “Imported Successfully”.
  1. Once imported successfully, go to the Contacts menu in Odoo.
  2. Search for the account you created in Salesforce — it should now be visible in Odoo.

Export Contacts/Accounts from Odoo to Salesforce

  1. First, open your Odoo Contacts.
  2. Click on a contact record to open the form view.
  3. At the top of the form, click on the gear icon labeled Action.
  4. From the dropdown, select the option Export to Salesforce.

Import Products from Salesforce to Odoo

  1. First, open Salesforce and go to the Products section.
  2. Click on All Product Lists.
  3. Create a new product and click Save.
  4. Now, open Odoo and go to the Salesforce menu.
  5. Under the Products tab, click on Import.
  6. Once the import is complete, a message will appear showing Imported Successfully.

Export Products from Odoo to Salesforce

  1. First, open your Odoo Products.
  2. Click on a Product record to open the form view.
  3. At the top of the form, click on the gear icon labeled Action.
  4. From the dropdown, select the option Export to Salesforce.

Import Opportunities from Salesforce to Odoo

  1. First, open Salesforce and go to the Opportunities section.
  2. Create a new opportunity, fill in the necessary fields, and save the record.
  3. Then, open Odoo, go to the Salesforce menu, and click on the Opportunities tab.
  4. Click on the Import button.
  5. Once the import is complete, a message will appear showing Imported Successfully.

Export Opportunities from Odoo to Salesforce

  1. First, open your Odoo CRM module.
  2. Click on the Opportunities menu and open an opportunity record.
  3. At the top of the form view, click on the gear icon labeled Action.
  4. From the dropdown, select the option Export to Salesforce.

Import Sale Quotations from Salesforce to Odoo

  1. Open Salesforce and navigate to the Quotes section.
  2. Create a new quote, fill in the required details, and save the record.
  3. Add products to the quote, enter the quantities for each product, and save it again. You can also upload any necessary attachments. Then, click the Mark Status as Complete button.
  4. Next, go to Odoo, open the Salesforce menu, and select the Quotations tab.
  5. Click the Import button.
  6. After the import is complete, a confirmation message saying Imported Successfully will appear.

Import Events from Salesforce to Odoo

  1. First, open Salesforce and go to the Events section.
  2. Create a new Event, fill in the necessary fields, and save the record.
  3. Then, open Odoo, go to the Salesforce menu, and click on the Events tab.
  4. Click on the Import button.
  5. Once the import is complete, a message will appear showing Imported Successfully.

Export Events from Odoo to Salesforce

  1. First, open your Odoo Calendar module.
  2. Click on the New menu and open a record.
  3. At the top of the form view, click on the gear icon labeled Action.
  4. From the dropdown, select the option Export to Salesforce.
